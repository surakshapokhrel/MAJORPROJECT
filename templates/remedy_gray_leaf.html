Remedies for Gray Leaf Spot

  • Plant Resistant Varieties: Choose corn hybrids that are resistant to gray leaf spot to minimize the risk of infection.
  • Rotate Crops: Rotate corn with non-host crops like soybeans or small grains to reduce disease buildup in the soil.
  • Manage Residue: Remove or bury crop debris after harvest to reduce overwintering spores that can infect the next season's crop.
  • Use Fungicides: Apply fungicides (like strobilurins or triazoles) early when you first notice the disease to control its spread.
  • Plant at Recommended Density: Avoid overcrowding. Proper spacing between plants improves airflow and reduces leaf wetness, making it harder for the disease to thrive.
  • Monitor Regularly: Scout your fields frequently, especially during humid weather, to catch and manage the disease early.
