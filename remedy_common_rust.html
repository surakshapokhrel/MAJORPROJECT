Remedy for Common Rust

  • Plant Smart Corn
    Choose corn varieties that resist rust. These are bred to make your life easier.
  • Change Things Up
    Don’t grow corn in the same spot every year. Rust can hide in the soil, so rotate with crops like beans or wheat.
  • Don’t Pack Them In
    Give your corn plants space to breathe. Crowded plants trap moisture, and rust loves wet leaves.
  • Use Fungicides (When Needed)
    If you see reddish-brown spots, act fast! Use fungicides like azoxystrobin or propiconazole. Follow the label for instructions.
  • Water Wisely
    Aim your watering at the base of the plants. Wet leaves create a perfect environment for rust.
  • Clean Up the Mess
    After harvest, remove old plants. Rust can survive on dead leaves and infect your next crop. Burn or bury them.
